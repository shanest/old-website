Instructions

Welcome to this experiment. In the next 30 minutes, you will be presented with pairs of sentences and pictures. Your task is to decide if the sentence is true or false based on the information from the picture.

The experiment will take approximately 30 minutes and you will be paid 4$. Try to do this task fast, but also as accurately as possible.

Sentences and pictures will appear on a separate screen. First, you will see a blank screen. Then, press the K button AND KEEP IT PRESSED. Keep the K button pressed as long as you need to read the sentence on the first screen, and release the button when you are ready to move to next screen.

To move to the second screen, the K down button (but you do not need to hold it). On this screen, you will be shown a picture, and also asked to indicate if the sentence is true or false.

Once you are sure about your answer, press the for TRUE and for FALSE.

Press the K button to advance to a quick training period, before the full experiment begins.
